Opening the Winery Facility

  1. Unlock front, side, and patio doors.
  2. Go to utility closet in front and turn on all 6 light switches.
  3. Turn all 3 sets of kitchen lights on.
  4. Check bathrooms for cleanliness and supply.
  5. Turn slushy machine from standby to slushy setting.
  6. Turn lights on in wine cooler and pop cooler.
  7. Clean glass doors and tables.
  8. In summer, complete these actions.
    1. Wipe outside tables and chairs.
    2. If necessary, add water to water fountain.
    3. Water any plants that require it.
  9. Stock supplies.
    1. Stock wine.
    2. Stock beer.
    3. Stock pop, food, and water.
  10. Turn Sonos music system on.
    See the Sonos topic for more info.
